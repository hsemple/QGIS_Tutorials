Welcome to QGIS Tutorials!
==========================================


Welcome to these tutorials which provide an introduction to the QGIS software. The tutorials are pitched at the level of an of introductory college level GIS course.  

You don't need to know anything about GIS to begin this course.  This is a completely beginner-friendly course. Even if you have some GIS experience, you will be sure find some of these tutorials useful.  

Topics covered by the different tutorials are shown in the Table of Content to the left of the page.
